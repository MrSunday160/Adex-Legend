Queens Canyon - Battle Royale

The island known as Queens Canyon didn't thrive until the EMC chose it as a hub for research and development. It housed an airbase, a water treatment plant, and a plethora of other facilities to support the EMC’s operations. Years later, when the Syndicate chose Queens Canyon as the site of their newest bloodsport, which then became the pinnacle of thriving excitement.

Storm Block - Battle Royale

Storm Block was the prime candidate for early EMC expeditions to the Klingon’s territory. However, EMC settlement teams quickly discovered that intense tropical storms plague the island , and settlements was soon abandoned; until it caught the eye of the Syndicate. Now, Storm Block becomes the latest Adex Games and thriving with wildlife and beasts.

Ilympus - Arena

Ilympus was a utopia floating in clouds above Neutron. Here, the brightest minds in the Outlands could gather and exchange ideas, leading to breakthroughs in the sciences and arts. From a prosper living envirnoment, Hammond Robotics stepped in to transform Olympus into a new stage for the newset Adex Game mode, Arena, becoming the leading city in shaping the future.
